Hollow Knight

A Metroidvania That Changed Everything

Overview Gameplay Lore

Game Overview

Hollow Knight is a critically acclaimed 2D action-adventure game that combines challenging combat, intricate platforming, and atmospheric exploration. Play as a mysterious knight exploring the vast underground kingdom of Hallownest, uncovering secrets and facing formidable foes in this beautifully hand-drawn world.

Gameplay

Master fluid combat with your trusty nail as you slash, dash, and spell your way through Hallownest's dangers. The gameplay revolves around precise platforming and timing-based combat, where every enemy encounter requires strategy and skill. Collect Soul from defeated foes to cast powerful spells or heal your wounds, managing this precious resource carefully as you delve deeper into increasingly treacherous areas.

Lore

Long ago, the Pale King ruled over the thriving kingdom of Hallownest, inhabited by intelligent bugs who built a great civilization beneath the surface. But an ancient force known as the Radiance brought forth an infection that consumed the minds of Hallownest's citizens, reducing them to mindless husks driven by orange light.

In desperation, the Pale King attempted to create a perfect vessel, a being without mind, will, or voice, to contain the infection. You play as the Knight, one such vessel, returning to the now ruined kingdom to confront the source of the plague. Piece together the tragic history through environmental storytelling, cryptic dialogue, and hidden lore tablets as you uncover the truth behind Hallownest's fall and your own mysterious origins.
